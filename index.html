The Wars

The Wars of the Roses is the popular name given to a series of dynastic civil wars in mid-fifteenth century England.

  • First War: 1455-1464
  • Second War: 1469-1471
  • Third War: 1483-1487

Yet even this simple definition is subject to much bitter dispute between historians of the period. This website seeks to offer a reference point and a spring board for any wishing to dip their toe into this rather exciting period of history.
